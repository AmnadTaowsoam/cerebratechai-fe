MLOps Best Practices (Outline)
-----------------------------
- Version everything: data, code, models, prompts/configs
- Reproducible pipelines: training + evaluation + deployment
- Monitoring: latency, errors, drift, quality signals
- Clear ownership: on-call, runbooks, escalation paths
- Safe releases: canary, shadow, rollback
- Security basics: least privilege, secrets management, audit logs
- Cost controls: budgets, rate limits, batching/caching where appropriate
